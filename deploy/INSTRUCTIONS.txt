# InfinityFree Deployment Instructions

This `deploy` folder contains your project prepared for shared hosting.

## Structure
- `/htdocs/` -> Contains the static assets and the entry point for your application.
- `/laravel/` -> Contains the core Laravel code.

## Steps for Upload:
1. Zip the **contents** of `htdocs` and upload them to the `htdocs` folder on your InfinityFree account (or the root folder of your domain).
2. Zip the **contents** of `laravel` and upload them to a folder named `laravel` ONE LEVEL ABOVE `htdocs` (in your account's home directory).
   - If you cannot upload above `htdocs`, put the `laravel` folder inside `htdocs` and update `index.php` manually.

## Database & Environment:
1. Open `.env` in the `laravel` folder.
2. Fill in your database details provided by InfinityFree:
   - `DB_HOST`: The MySQL hostname from InfinityFree.
   - `DB_DATABASE`: The database name you created.
   - `DB_USERNAME`: Your InfinityFree username.
   - `DB_PASSWORD`: Your hosting account password.
3. Import your local database SQL into InfinityFree via phpMyAdmin.

## Optimization:
- Run `php artisan config:cache` if you have terminal access (not usually available on InfinityFree).
- Make sure `/laravel/storage` and `/laravel/bootstrap/cache` are writable (775 or similar).

Congratulations! Your Rose Galerie is ready to shine on the web! ✨👠🚀
